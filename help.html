Help

If you are having issues taking good photos here are some tips and tricks to get better results from our AI, Onnx:

- Make sure the plant is in clear and even lighting.
- Get a clear angle of the plant.
- Move plant into an area away from other plants.
- Try take photos from multiple angles and see if one of them works.

If you have tried using our tips and tricks on how to take good photos and Onnx is still not detecting images, here are some ideas you can try:
- Uploading an image of the plant using our upload button.
- If you have an idea about what the name of the plant is, try searching it up in our database.
- If neither of these work, try take a look through our database and see if there are any plants that seem to fit.
- Please contact us about any gaps in Onnx's image training contact us at: Leafmatch.app@gmail.com
